Petroleum Logistics
Rooted in Rwanda

Established in 2017, KATRACO Rwanda Ltd is the regional subsidiary of KATRACO (U) LTD — a company with over 34 years of proven petroleum transport excellence across East and Central Africa.


Operating from Kigali, we provide dependable fuel transportation services to major petroleum marketers, fuel distributors, and industrial clients throughout Rwanda and the broader EAC region. Our Rwanda operations are part of the MFK logistics group network.
